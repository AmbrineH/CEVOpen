In this study we used six samples of red propolis collected in different regions of Brazil and two extraction methods were used to obtain the extracts, totaling 12 extracts. Number 1 after the letter means the conventional method of extraction and the number 2 after the letter means the method with the application of the ultrasound.

A1, B1, C1, D1, E1 and F1—Extracts obtained by conventional extraction; A2, B2, C2, D2, E2 and F2—Extracts obtained by ultrasound-assisted extraction.

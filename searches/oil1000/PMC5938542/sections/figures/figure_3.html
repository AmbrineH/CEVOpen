HCT-116 cells were seeded in 6-well plates 1.0 ×10 3. After 24 h, cells were treated with (25, 50 and 100 μg/mL) concentrations of PREO. After 48 hours, the medium containing essential oil was washed off; the cells were allowed to form colonies for 12 days. Stained with 0.5 % crystal violet solution, washed once with PBS, air-dried, and counted.

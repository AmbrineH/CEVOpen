Log abundance of bacterial orders in two exposure groups of Atlantic cod. Black asterisks denote statistical significance as determined by MetaStats ( p < 0.05). The grey asterisks represent orders that in a Wilcoxon rank-sum test have a p-value < 0.05 but is still above the Bonferroni corrected p-value (0.0045).

Linear regression curve for growth inhibitory activity of different doses of (A) H. spicatum L. essential oil (HSEO) and (B) radiation treatments on F. graminearum in maize. The log CFU/g was declined with the dose of HSEO and radiation. The data was processed by one-way ANOVA following Tukey's test and value of p < 0.05 was considered significant.

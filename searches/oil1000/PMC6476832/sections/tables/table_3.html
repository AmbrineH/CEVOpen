Estimated active and total time consumption (h/sample) together with advantages and disadvantages for different treatment methods with wastewater (W) and sludge (S) samples
Treatment method Active time (h/sample) Total time (h/sample) Advantages (+) Disadvantages (−)

KOH W: 2.0 S: 1.5 W: 50.0 S: 50.0 + Simple procedure + No visible deformations − Cellulose fibres and other organic material not removed − Organic material attached to MPs; may affect identification − High chemical consumption (alkaline) − Filters clogged quickly

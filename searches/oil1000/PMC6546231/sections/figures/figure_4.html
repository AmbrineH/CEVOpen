Food consumption of puppies (A—males; B—females) after weaning. Values are shown as group means (n = 5) in grams per dog per day. Statistically significant differences were observed between the mid-dose and control males on Weeks 7, 12, 14 and 18 (p<0.1). Statistically significant differences were observed between the mid-dose and control females on Weeks 3 and 14 (p<0.1), and between the high-dose and control groups on Weeks 15, 19, and 24 (p<0.1).

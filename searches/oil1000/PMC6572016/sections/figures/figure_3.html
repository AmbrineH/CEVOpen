Four-channel fluorescence of thyme/clove oil at ½ MIC: ( G) Transmission Channel, ( H) Red Channel, ( I) Blue Channel, ( J) Green Channel, ( K) stack of Red-Blue-Green Channels, ( L) cross-section of Red-Blue-Green Channels stack (green, blue and red fluorescence signals in vacuoles).

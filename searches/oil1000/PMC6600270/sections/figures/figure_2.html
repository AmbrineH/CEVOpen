Pineapple volatile profiles (GC×GC-quadrupole MS (qMS)) at different ripening stages: ( a) FR 2 dah; ( b) GR 2 dah; and ( c) GR 24 dah. Principal component analysis score plots ( d) PC1 × PC2 and ( e) PC1 × PC3. GR: green-ripe; FR: fully ripe; dah: days after harvest.

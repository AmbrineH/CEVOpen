Effect of the dietary inclusion of Hermetia illucens larvae on the concentration of lactate ( a) and short-chain fatty acid (SCFA) ( b) in the colonic digesta of pigs. The values are means ± SEM ( n = 8). Asterisks indicated statistical differences between different group (One-way ANOVA with a Tukey post hoc test): * P < 0.05.

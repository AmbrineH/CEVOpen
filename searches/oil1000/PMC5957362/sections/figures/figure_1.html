Viability of HEK293T cells infected with RRV-T48. A: without treatment by the essential oils; B: upon treatment with the essential oil at the concentration 1×CC 10 at 24, 32 or 48 h post-infection. Controls (CTL) consist in untreated cells with the essential oils and results are expressed as mean ± SEM (n = 5). Statistical analysis was performed with Prism 7 (* p < 0.05, ** p < 0.01, *** p < 0.005).

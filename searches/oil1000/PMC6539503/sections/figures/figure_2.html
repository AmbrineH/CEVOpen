Produced water and discharged dispersed oil from the Danish platforms in the North Sea. The platforms include: Dan, Gorm, Halfdan, Tyra, Syd Arne and Siri. The fields are operated by Total E&P Danmark A/S, Hess Danmark ApS and INEOS Oil & Gas. The blue graph illustrates the total amount of produced water from the fields. The red graph illustrates the discharged dispersed oil in the PW.

Antibacterial activity of herbal toothpastes before and after essential oil addition. Same capital letters and same coloured columns indicate no statistically significant difference ( p > 0.05). Different capital letters and different coloured columns indicate statistically significant differences ( p < 0.05). y-axis shows the inhibition zones of the toothpastes against S. mutans. (JJ: Jack N’ Jill; SO: Splat Organic; SB: Splat Biocalcium; JJ + OD: Jack N’ Jill + O. dubium essential oil; JJ + CC: Jack N’ Jill + C. cassia essential oil; SO + OD: Splat Organic + O. dubium essential oil; SO + CC: Splat Organic + C. cassia essential oil; B + OD: Biocalcium + O. dubium essential oil; B + CC: Biocalcium+ C. cassia essential oil).

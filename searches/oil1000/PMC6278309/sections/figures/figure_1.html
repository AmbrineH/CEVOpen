Main chemical structures found in the Tagetes essential oils (EOs): ( 1) limonene, ( 2) α-pinene, ( 3) β-pinene, ( 4) terpinolene, ( 5) ( E)-β-ocimene, ( 6) dihydrotagetone, ( 7) tagetone (represented as a mixture of ( E)- and ( Z)-isomers), ( 8) tagetenone (represented as a mixture of ( E)- and ( Z)-isomers), ( 9) β-caryophyllene, and ( 10) eugenol.

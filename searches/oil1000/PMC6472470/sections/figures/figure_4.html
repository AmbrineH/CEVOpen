Effect of various oily carriers on the activity of isononyl isononanoate to reduce the peak force required to move louse eggs on hairs. Key to treatments: ININ dry, isononyl isononanoate applied to dry hair; ININ wet, isononyl isononanoate applied to pre-wetted hair; ININ + 4%D, isononyl isononanoate mixed into Hedrin Once 4% dimeticone spray gel applied to dry hair; ININ + poly5, isononyl isononanoate + polyisobutene 5; ININ + poly30, isononyl isononanoate + polyisobutene 30; ININ PEG-8 dp, isononyl isononanoate + PEG-8 dimeticone phosphate; ININ + DDV/DC, isononyl isononanoate + bis-divinyl dimeticone/PEG-10 dimeticone crosspolymer. Treatments with the same letter have no significant difference p < 0.05.

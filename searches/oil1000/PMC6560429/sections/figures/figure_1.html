Changes (log cfu/g) in population of Total Viable Count (TVC) in chicken thigh stored in air (APCS, ♦); stored under vacuum (VPC, ■); stored under vacuum packaging with rapeseed oil (VPRO, ▲); stored under vacuum packaging with Foeniculum vulgare 0.2% essential oil (VP + F, × ); and stored under vacuum packaging with Satureja hortensis 0.2% essential oil (VP + S, ●). Each point is the mean of three samples taken from two replicate experiments ( n = 3 × 2 = 6). Error bars show SD.

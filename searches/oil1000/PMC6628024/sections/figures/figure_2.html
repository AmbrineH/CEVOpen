Scanning electron microscopy (SEM) micrographs of a female Sitophilus zeamais antenna. ( A) General view of the whole antenna. The apical club-like antennomere is clearly visible on the right side of the picture. ( B, C) detail of the apical antennomere in the lateral ( B) and apical ( C) view, respectively. The apical sensory area is clearly visible. F1, first antennomere of the funicle; F6, sixth antennomere of the funicle; Pe, pedicel; Sc, scape. Bar scale: ( A) 200 µm; ( B, C) 50 µm.

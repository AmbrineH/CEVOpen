Dose–response relationships for stimulation of male and female antennae of Sitophilus zeamais with Clinopodium nubigenum ( A) and C. tomentosum ( B) essential oils. Data (mean ± standard error) represent the EAG response relative to octanal (%). Table insets show the statistical parameters of two-way ANOVA. The response of the two gender to each compound is compared to those of the control (paraffin oil) (*, p < 0.05; ns, not significant; ANOVA, Dunnett post hoc test).

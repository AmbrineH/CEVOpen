In the liver, excess dietary fat-intake could accelerate the esterification of FFAs with the absence of FAs Î²-oxidation. Indeed, the extra uptake of FFAs could provide the mass Acyl-CoA substrate for lipogenesis via the up-regulation of srebp1.

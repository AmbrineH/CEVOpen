Factors governing the monolayer phase behavior.
Van der Waals interaction:

Short range forces: (Γ VDW ∝ r −6)

Directly proportional to the number of carbon atoms in the hydrocarbon chain for the saturated hydrocarbon

Depends on the presence of double bonds or substituents like methyl group in the chain
Electrostatic interaction:

Ion – Ion (Γ ii ∝ r −1)

Ion – Dipole (Γ id ∝ r −2)

Dipole – Dipole (Γ dd ∝ r −3)

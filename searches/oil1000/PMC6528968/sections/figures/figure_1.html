Fluorescence imaging. a) Representative confocal microscopy images of 3T3-L1 cells Undifferentiated or differentiated with cultured adipogenic media, fixed at day 5, day 10 and day 15 of the differentiation process, fixed and labeled with LipidTox Red (red), Oil Red O (red) or BODIPY 493/503 (green) fluorescent dyes.

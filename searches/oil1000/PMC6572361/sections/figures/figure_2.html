Mortality of A. obtectus in 0.33 L/glass jars exposed to beans treated with different doses of O. basilicum ( A) and C. winterianus ( B) essential oils. Different capital letters indicate significant differences among doses and control for the same day; LSD test at 0.05. The symbols represent the mean of four replicates for each dose. Vertical bars represent the Standard Error (SE) of the mean.

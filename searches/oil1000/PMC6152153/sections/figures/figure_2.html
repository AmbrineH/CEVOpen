Cultivation conditions for three basil pot cultures in the greenhouse of FH MENDELU during the years 2015–2016. Upper graph: changes in average temperatures. Middle graph: changes in relative humidity. Lower graph: changes in solar irradiance. The presented pot cultures (PC) correspond to the following seasons: PC 1 2015 (23.3.–1.6.), PC 1 2016 (8.2.–25.4.), PC 2 2015 (27.4.–14.7.), PC 2 2016 (28.4.–20.6.), PC 3 2015 (1.9.–26.10.), and PC 3 2016 (15.8.–17.10.).

Photomicrograph of different chick embryo chorioallantoic membranes (CAM) after 72 h of treatments with ( A), oEd 146 (oEd 146 μg/disk); ( B), 292 (oEd 292 μg/disk); ( C), oEd 584 (oEd 584 μg/disk); and ( D), olive oil (20 μL, negative control); ( E), DEXA (dexamethasone 80 μg/disk, angiogenesis inhibitor) and ( F) RG (Regederm ® 20 mg/disk, angiogenesis inductor). Evaluation of CAMs showed a proangiogenic response by macroscopic semiquantitative scoring. The images illustrate representative examples of different responses (score rangeaccording to Ribatti, D, et al. 2006 [ 40]), as observed under an Axiovert Light Microscope with the aid of the AxioVision 100 software (both Zeiss, Göttingen, Germany), at the magnification of 0.5 × 1.25.

Average beetle infestation of the wheat stored in the mini-silos. CW, control wheat; W+EO, wheat treated with the essential oil of Ocimum basilicum; W + DEs, wheat treated with diatomaceous earths; W + DEs + OE, wheat treated with O. basilicum essential oil and diatomaceous earths. Data represent the mean number of adults kg −1 of grain. Bars represent the standard error. Different letters indicate significant differences among the treatments according to Tukey’s b post-hoc test ( p < 0.05).

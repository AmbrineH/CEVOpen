Landing reduction time course analysis for Aedes aegypti mosquitoes. Time course analysis comparing the landing reduction percentages of Aedes aegypti mosquitoes with 10% DEET and crude CR9 essential oil treatments over 24 hours. The samples were tested at 0 hour, 1 hour, 2 hours, 4 hours, 8 hours and 24 hours after application of the treatments. Values are presented as means ± standard deviation (SD). Data was analyzed by an unpaired, two-tailed student’s t-test to identify significant differences (P < 0.05).

Landing reduction dose-response for Aedes aegypti mosquitoes. Dose response landing reduction for Aedes aegypti using DEET, N. cataria CR3 crude essential oil, N. cataria cv. ‘CR9’ crude essential oil, a commercial N. cataria essential oil, purified Z, E-nepetalactone and E, Z-nepetalactone treatments at 0.01%, 0.10% and 1.00%. Values are presented as means ± standard deviation (SD). Data was analyzed by an unpaired, two-tailed student’s t-test to identify significant differences (P < 0.05).

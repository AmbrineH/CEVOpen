Percentage repellency (PR) of Asarum heterotropoides essential oil (EO) against Lasioderma serricorne and Liposcelis bostrychophila at 2 h and 4 h post-exposure. Means in the same column followed by the same letters do not differ significantly ( p > 0.05) in ANOVA and Tukey’s tests. PR was subjected to an arcsine square-root transformation before ANOVA and Tukey’s tests. ER and EL: the essential oils from roots and leaves of A. heterotropoides, respectively.

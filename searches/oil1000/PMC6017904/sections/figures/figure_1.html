Effect of EOs from Foeniculum vulgare Miller (FV) ( A), Calamintha nepeta (L.) Savi subsp. glandulosa (Req.) Ball (CG) ( B), and Ridolfia segetum Moris (RS) ( C) on biofilm formation of P. aeruginosa PaO1.

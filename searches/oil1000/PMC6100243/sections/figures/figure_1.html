Principal component analysis biplot of essential oil components from B. scorzonerifolium samples collected from different regions. Numerals indicate own data ( Table 1). Light-blue-filled quadrants indicate samples from Buryatia (Russia), green-filled quadrant is the sample from the Trans–Baikal region (Russia), brown-filled quadrants are the samples from Khentii aimag (Mongolia). Brown-filled circle (mng2010) is according to [ 10]. “Irkutsk” indicates the B. scorzonerifolium sample from the Irkutsk region (Russia) according to [ 9]. “Krasnoyarsk” indicates the B. scorzonerifolium sample from the Krasnoyarsk region (Russia) according to [ 7, 8].
